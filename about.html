About Thalos

Our Vision

At Thalos, our vision is to integrate medical expertise, sports science, and digital health through advanced artificial intelligence. We aim to create a new generation of personalized health and fitness solutions that amplify human potential.

To us, "engineering performance" means leveraging cutting-edge technology to enhance the body's strengths and bypass its weaknesses. By deeply hacking the human body with data-driven insights, we tailor training, recovery, and nutrition to unlock peak performance for every individual.

Key Technologies

  • AI Coaching: An adaptive AI coach that delivers personalized workout and recovery plans, adjusting to your progress in real time.
  • Biometric Tracking: Continuous monitoring via wearable sensors (heart rate, sleep patterns, stress levels) providing 24/7 insights.
  • Personalized Nutrition: Customized diet and supplement strategies aligned to your goals, dietary preferences, and biological needs.
  • Genomic & Biomarker Analysis: Integration of genetic profiling and biomarker data (blood work, microbiome analysis, etc.) for truly individualized recommendations.
