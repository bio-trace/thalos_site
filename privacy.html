2. What data we collect

Depending on how you use our Services, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

A) Data you provide to us

  • Account data (e.g., name, email address, login credentials)

(Comparable fitness apps explicitly list workout/fitness/body data as user-provided personal data; Thalos will likely fall into the same category.)

B) Data collected automatically

  • Device & usage data (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages viewed, interactions, timestamps)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (e.g., necessary cookies, analytics cookies if enabled)

(MyFitnessPal also lists usage/device data and cookie-based collection as standard.)

C) Data from third parties (optional)

If you connect Thalos to third-party services in the future (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit, wearables), we may receive related fitness information depending on your permissions. (Only applies if you offer such integrations.)

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • AI coaching & personalization
  • Generate personalised workout plans

(Apps offering AI coaching typically describe personalization as a core processing purpose.)

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): to provide the Services you request
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): for optional features (e.g., marketing emails, analytics cookies)
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): service security, fraud prevention, product improvement
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): when we must retain data by law

5. Health / fitness data

Data such as your workouts, fitness performance, nutrition logs, and body-related metrics may be considered health-related data depending on context.

We process such data strictly to provide the Services and personalize your training experience. Where required, we process sensitive data only with your explicit consent (Art. 9 GDPR) or as otherwise permitted by applicable law.

Important: Thalos does not provide medical advice. Always consult a medical professional before starting a training or nutrition program.

6. How AI processing works

Thalos uses AI models to generate training, nutrition, and supplement guidance based on the information you provide (e.g., goals, training history, preferences).

We may process your inputs to create recommendations and plans.

We do not intentionally request sensitive information unrelated to fitness (e.g., political views).

You remain responsible for what you input and for applying recommendations safely.

AI Training Note (recommended): Unless explicitly stated otherwise, we do not use your private workout/nutrition content to train public AI models. If this changes, we will clearly disclose it and request consent where required.
